More about Statista

I spend my 2 weeks internship at the Revenue Tech department at the Headquarter of Statista, where I learned to code and designed this website on my own. It is important to know, that Statista is a Company, which provides people and other Companies with data. Statista has 8 offices all around the world. The Headquarter is located in Hamburg with over 1.200 employees, where I completed my internship.

Brahms-Kontor

The Brahms-Kontor" is an historically important building, in the center of Hamburg, next to the park "Planten un Bloomen". With 12 floors, it was once the tallest building of Hamburg. Furthermore, it was named after the famous composer Johannes Brahms, who once lived there and has already served as a filming location for famous series and movies, such as the "Tatortreiniger". Nowadays, it is the Headquarter of Statista and my internship workplace.
